About RKR Construction Services

Our Story

R.K.R established in 2022, is a testament to the power of family and the pursuit of a dream. The company's name, derived from the initials of the owner's children - Richmond, Katie, and Rica - underscores the personal investment and familial values at the core of the business.

R.K.R's journey commenced in Lucban, Quezon, with the launch of a Woodworks business in January. This venture was the first step towards realizing the proprietor's vision and laid the foundation for the company's growth and diversification.

Shortly after establishing the Woodworks business, R.K.R expanded its operations to include a Nature Park in the same town. This Park, with its prime location, quickly became a haven for campers and adventure seekers, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and thrilling experiences.

R.K.R's ambition didn't stop there. In October of the same year, eager to explore new opportunities and venture into a larger business setup, the company launched its Aggregates and Construction Supply division. This move marked a significant milestone in R.K.R’s journey, propelling it into a highly competitive and demanding industry.

In 2023, R.K.R further expanded its operations by launching R.K.R Construction Services. This addition demonstrated the company's commitment to providing comprehensive solutions in the construction industry.

Despite the challenges, R.K.R has carved out a strong position for itself in the construction sector. Today, it is one of the most sought-after contractors for both private and government projects. This achievement is a testament to the company's relentless pursuit of excellence, its commitment to delivering high-quality products and services, and its ability to build strong, lasting relationships with its clients.

R.K.R's story is a powerful reminder that with passion, perseverance, and a clear vision, it's possible to transform a small family business into a thriving enterprise. As R.K.R continues to grow and evolve, it carries with it the dreams and aspirations of Richmond, Katie, and Rica, and the enduring legacy of a family's love and dedication.
